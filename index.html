    • Hall for hire

    Situated in an acre of green land, set back from the road, in the picturesque village of Woodchurch.

    “Our hall has loads of room inside and out, and we keep it tidy so that other people can enjoy it when we're not using it.”

    Daniel Mines - 1st Woodchurch Scout Group

    Outside

    A place to relax - well established trees, and grassy areas suitable for recreation.

    The Summer house is often used for small meetings and events.

    Plenty of parking - easily enough for 20+ vehicles.

    Inside

    There's lots of space - the main hall area is 10m wide x 17m long.

    Enter the hall through a glass conservortory – 4m x 5m.

    Ladies, gents and disabled toilets.

    Plenty of room to prepare food.

    The kitchen is equipped with:
    • Gas cooker
    • Fridge
    • Freezer
    • Kettle
    • Microwave
    • Hand-sink
    • and a Kitchen-sink!
    • Plates, Bowls, Cups
    • Cutlery and Utensils

    Location

    Peaceful location with beautiful surroundings, situated in the village of Woodchurch.
